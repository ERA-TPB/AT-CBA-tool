### Demand growth rates

Demand estimates also need to be projected into the future either by using a fixed annual growth rate or by using a demand model.

Where a demand model is not used, fixed annual growth rates are assumed over the appraisal period. Plausibility checks on future demand should be used to ensure these assumptions are realistic.
